Personal Projects

Connect Four in MonoGame
This is a project I started working on in Intro to Computer Science and finished in roughly a week. MonoGame is an extension of Microsoft XNA Framework, as XNA was officially discontinued in 2014. 3 games that I enjoy all come from XNA or Monogame, so I enjoy working with those libraries. The actual game I made was basic, however it fully functions and is available on Github. I want to dip my toes into making a bot to play against, but that would be very difficult and time consuming. I'll save that for outside of school ;)
Giant's Deep Clock
This is how I've used ECMAScript-related languages before. There is an application called Wallpaper Engine that allows for the creation and distribution of live wallpapers, and HTML/CSS/JS wallpapers. The wallpaper is based on the game Outer Wilds, and required me to make custom circular path logic using a wallpaper equivalent of Javascript. The wallpaper is available for download on Steam assuming you have Wallpaper Engine ($5.99).
Stardew Valley Crop Calculator
This is just a spreadsheet that calculates the daily currency per day of some crops in the game Stardew Valley. There's two hotly contested choices for the best crop: Ancient Fruit, or Starfruit. Ancient Fruit regrows on its own and is harvested every 7 days, but takes 1 month to initially grow and sells for less. Starfruit takes 13 days to grow, but sells for more and needs to be replanted after harvesting. With the calculations I made, Ancient Fruit is the better choice.
